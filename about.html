MISSION STATEMENT

BREVERSE is a small business based in Ontario, Canada, founded by Bre Ramjit. Bre has always had a passion for art and design and is delighted to be able to share her creations with others. BREVERSE aims to create handmade pieces that are minimalist and chic. Since all items are handmade, no two items are exactly the same which offers a unique and personal touch!

BREVERSE values inclusivity and creates jewelry for individuals of all gender identities and expressions.

Thank you for stopping by, the support means the world!

MEET THE CREATOR
